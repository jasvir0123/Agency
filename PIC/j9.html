Rise from THE ASHES, RECLAIM A SHATTERED WORLD.

Thrilling open-world survival game set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by catastrophic volcanic eruptions.

Get the Game

Immersive Open World

Explore a vast and desolate wasteland teeming with hidden dangers, unique landmarks, and unexpected encounters. Uncover remnants of lost civilizations, hidden bunkers, and long-forgotten relics of the old world, each providing clues to the events that led to humanity's downfall.

A truly gripping story of humanity & courage!

Jerry Hsu

Gaming Magazine

Award Winning

Unravel a rich and gripping story as you interact with fellow survivors, each with their own unique personalities, stories, and motivations. Make choices that will affect the world around you, and uncover the dark truth behind the Ashfall.

Deep Crafting System

In Ashfall, the robust crafting system is the key to survival in a world that has been reduced to rubble. Scavenge through the remnants of the old world to find valuable resources, materials, and components, which you can repurpose to create weapons, armor, tools, and settlements tailored to your playstyle and the challenges you face.

Custom Weapons and Armor

Construct and upgrade crafting stations, such as workbenches, forges, and chemistry labs, to unlock new crafting recipes and enhance your equipment.

Settlement Building

Establish and expand your settlements by constructing various buildings, such as shelters, farms, and defensive structures.

Blueprints and Technologies

Scour the wasteland to discover rare blueprints, which reveal advanced crafting recipes and powerful technologies long thought lost to the apocalypse.
